Your privacy is important to us. We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the App depends on the content and materials you use, and includes:

  • Personal Data: Personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, and demographic information (such as your age, gender, hometown, and interests), that you voluntarily give to us when you register with the App.
  • Cycle Data: Information you provide about your menstrual cycle, including cycle length, period dates, symptoms, and other related health notes. This data is highly sensitive and we treat it with the utmost care.
  • Derivative Data: Information our servers automatically collect when you access the App, such as your IP address, your browser type, your operating system, your access times, and the pages you have viewed directly before and after accessing the App.
